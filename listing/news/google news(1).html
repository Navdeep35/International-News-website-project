Google Fit will now track your sleep patterns


Now, you can use Google Fit to track your sleep patterns.


Google Fit now tracks users’ sleep patterns from applications that share their data, thus, giving them a better sense of increasing or cutting their sleeping hours. Users can also add and edit their sleep history if companion apps aren’t providing a complete picture of the downtime. “With the revamped Fit last year, Google focused on physical activity through Heart Points and Move Minutes.
